About

Hi, I'm Lisa Dean! This is my web development portfolio where I share all of my favorite work. In the third grade, my teacher video-taped our class as we each stood up and stated our favorite hobby. When it was my turn I said, “I like to make stories on computers.” At the time I knew nothing of web development, but it became a prediction of my future passion. I want to translate your “story” into a web presence.

I am a multi-disciplinary developer with experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, copywriting, editing, and design. When I'm not working I enjoy spending time with family, cooking, reading novels, and taking barre classes.
